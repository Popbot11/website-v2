old wip dump (2021 - 2024)

this is a big playlist of stuff from between 2021 and 2024 that I never did anything with. most of these are really small ideas that never were worked on for more than a couple hours, maybe days. There's a few "completed" or more developed tracks in here as well that were made mostly for challenges or similar that I didnt end up releasing publically for whatever reason. Looking back, a few of the more complete ideas are actually very cool and I might end up doing something with them in the future, but for now they only exist like this.
in 2021 and 2022 I obviously had a LOT of free time and started way more ideas than I finished. I was a high schooler and literally had nothing else to do-- almost no normal social life. now as im updating this page in march 2025 I start things way less frequently and am more focused on actually finishing things; I like to think that my time managment skills have progressed in a positive direction.
but yea i mean i dont want these things to just dissappear forever cause some of them are kinda interesting, at the very least. it's kinda fun to skim through since most are under a minute.
